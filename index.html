What parts should a Gaming PC have?

1. Case - So you can put your parts inside

2. Motherboard - Make sure everything is compatible with it!

3. CPU - Without a CPU a PC doesn't work, The CPU is the brain of the PC

4. RAM - How will your background apps run without RAM?

5. M2/SSD/HDD - A gaming pc should preferably have an SSD or M2 and for additional storage an HDD

6 GPU - Transforms the CPU's orders to images

7 PSU - How will your pc get power with out a Power Supply Unit???

8 CPU Cooler - Like for real, do you want your PC to run normally without a cooler

9 Thermal-paste - Helps transfering the heat from the IHS to the cooler
